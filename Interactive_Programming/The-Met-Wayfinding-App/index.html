The Met Wayfinder

This app will make your visit easier by mapping a direct route through the museum. Select the exhibitions you want to see from the drop down menu on the next page by tapping on an exhibition name and tapping “Add to route.” Once you have selected your exhibitions, tap “Make My Route!” We will show you the most direct path to the exhibitions you have chosen, and you will be able to modify the route to fit your needs.
